Effective ventilation repair

VTAPE provides safe, fast and effective patching of holes in underground ventilation duct


  • PERFORMANCE - VTAPE provides a long-term fix for minor ventilation damage
  • REAL OUTCOMES - Improve working conditions while saving time and money 
  • COST EFFECTIVE - Most holes in ventilation bag do not get repaired. Collectively they contribute to large pressure loss. With VTAPE these can be sealed closed without complicated repairs Try it now!

Small holes and large holes

VTAPE works on small holes such as shot bag or where cable bolts have rubbed through ventilation bag. It bonds to dry and damp ventilation bag extremely well.

Repair with the fan on or off

VTAPE can be applied and re-applied to reinforce areas that require more durability.

Sizing and Options

Small

Ideal for patching multiple small holes, especially with ventilation still operating. They are extremely cost effective and useful. 

Recommended max hole - 10cm

Medium

Medium patches cover 80% of the holes we experience in our ventilation bag. They are easily applied and work well.
