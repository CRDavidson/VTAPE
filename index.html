How VTAPE Works

Don't replace the bag

VTAPE works on small to medium holes such as shot bag or where cable bolts have rubbed through ventilation bag. It bonds to dry and damp duct extremely well.

Applied quickly 

VTAPE can be applied and re-applied to reinforce areas that require more durability. The fan can be running or switched off during application. Don't waste time with stitching and cable ties. Do it right, make it look good and get back to work.

Sizing Options

Small

Ideal for patching multiple small holes, especially with ventilation still operating. They are extremely cost effective and useful. 

Recommended max hole - 10cm

Medium

Medium patches cover 80% of the holes we experience in our ventilation bag. They are easily applied and work well.

Recommended max hole - 20cm

Large

Large patches cover multiple holes and tears in a specific area of the ventilation bag. Useful for large dedicated repairs.

Recommended max hole - 27cm

Extra Large

Extra Large patches are used for custom repair work and are great for reinforcing areas that wear down with truck rubbing. Best in low pressure areas (+200m from the fan).
